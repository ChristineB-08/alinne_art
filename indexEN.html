Glass Drawing

Imagine holding a transparent glass surface, just waiting to be transformed into a work of art. With acrylic colors, every brushstroke becomes a whisper of creativity that blends with light, bringing images to life as if they were dancing on the glass. Every color, like a gentle caress, rests on the glossy surface, creating shades and light effects.
Glass, a cold and silent material, transforms under the artist's touch, becoming a stage for stories and emotions. Once the drawing takes shape, the glass is no longer just glass. It becomes a living, breathing, storytelling object.

Anime Characters Who Breathe on Glass

On the glass, the brush weaves silence, where the stroke is not just a hand, but a thought taking shape.
Each drawing is a breath suspended between the world and the idea, a soul born from nothing, trapped in the crystal.
